All presale contributions were denominated in USD. The amount of ANT calculated is how many ANT could have been bought with x USD in the preallocation day.

ANT amount will be calculated on allocation day using the following formula:

`ANT = 120 * USD_contribution / ETH_price`

(Ether price will be current price on GDAX)
